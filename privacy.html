Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how AdriaticFerry ("the App"), developed by SoftSplit d.o.o., handles your information. We are committed to protecting your privacy and being transparent about our practices.

1. Information We Collect

AdriaticFerry does not collect any personal data. We do not require account creation, login, or any personal information to use the App.

2. Location Data

The App may request access to your device's location. This is optional and used only to identify the nearest port to your current position. Location data is:

  • Used only while the App is in use ("when-in-use" permission)
  • Processed entirely on your device
  • Never transmitted to any server or third party
  • Never stored or logged

You can use the App without granting location permission. You can revoke location access at any time in your device's Settings.

3. AIS Vessel Data

The App connects to the aisstream.io WebSocket service to fetch real-time vessel positions. When using this feature:

  • Only vessel MMSI numbers (Maritime Mobile Service Identity) are sent to the service to request position data
  • No personal data, device identifiers, or user information is transmitted
  • MMSI numbers are public vessel identifiers, not personal data

4. Local Data Storage

All schedule data, port information, and route data is bundled locally within the App. This data:

  • Is included in the app download
  • Does not require an internet connection to access
  • Is removed when you delete the App

5. Subscriptions

The App offers optional auto-renewable subscriptions managed entirely by Apple through StoreKit. All payment processing is handled by Apple. SoftSplit d.o.o. does not have access to your payment information, Apple ID, or any billing details.

6. No Analytics or Tracking

AdriaticFerry does not use any analytics services, advertising SDKs, tracking tools, or data collection frameworks. We do not use cookies or similar technologies.

7. No Third-Party SDKs

The App does not include any third-party SDKs for advertising, analytics, or social media. The only external connection is to aisstream.io for vessel position data as described above.

8. Children's Privacy

The App does not collect personal information from anyone, including children under the age of 13. The App is suitable for all ages.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected on this page with an updated effective date.
