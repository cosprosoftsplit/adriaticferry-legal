Frequently Asked Questions

How does vessel tracking work?

AdriaticFerry uses AIS (Automatic Identification System) data to show real-time vessel positions. AIS signals are broadcast by vessels and received by shore stations along the Adriatic coast. The App connects to the aisstream.io service to receive these positions and display them on the map.

Why can't I see a vessel's position?

There are several reasons a vessel may not appear on the map:

  • The vessel may be in an area with limited AIS shore station coverage, especially on mid-Adriatic routes
  • The vessel's AIS transponder may be temporarily offline or malfunctioning
  • There may be a delay in position reporting from the AIS network
  • The vessel may not yet have departed or may have already arrived

How many free lookups do I get?

Free users can perform 3 vessel tracking lookups per day. All ferry schedules, port information, and route details are always available for free. To get unlimited vessel tracking lookups, upgrade to AdriaticFerry Premium.

How do I cancel my subscription?

To cancel your AdriaticFerry Premium subscription:

  • Open the Settings app on your iPhone or iPad
  • Tap your name (Apple ID) at the top
  • Tap Subscriptions
  • Find AdriaticFerry and tap Cancel Subscription

Your premium access will continue until the end of the current billing period.

Do schedules update automatically?

Ferry schedules are bundled with the App and are updated with each new App version. We recommend keeping the App updated to ensure you have the latest schedule data. Check for updates in the App Store regularly, especially before the summer season when schedules change.

Does the App work offline?

Yes, partially. All ferry schedules, port information, and route details work offline without an internet connection. However, real-time vessel tracking requires an active internet connection to receive AIS position data.

Are the schedules guaranteed to be accurate?

We compile schedule data from publicly available sources and strive for accuracy, but schedules may change due to weather, operator decisions, or seasonal adjustments. Always verify schedules directly with the ferry operator before travel, especially during off-season months.

The App is not working properly. What should I do?

Try the following steps:

  • Make sure you have the latest version of the App installed
  • Check your internet connection (required for vessel tracking)
  • Restart the App
  • Restart your device
  • Reinstall the App

If the issue persists, please contact us at info@softsplit.hr with a description of the problem.
