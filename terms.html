1. Description of Service

AdriaticFerry is a ferry schedule and vessel tracking application for the Adriatic Sea. The App provides ferry schedules, port information, route details, and real-time vessel positions via AIS (Automatic Identification System) data.

2. Schedule Data Disclaimer

Ferry schedule data provided in the App is compiled from publicly available information and may not reflect real-time changes made by ferry operators. Schedules may be subject to seasonal changes, weather-related cancellations, or other modifications without notice. Users should always verify schedules directly with the ferry operator before travel.

3. AIS Data Disclaimer

Real-time vessel positions are provided on a best-effort basis using AIS data from aisstream.io. This data may have:

  • Delays in position reporting
  • Coverage gaps, especially on mid-Adriatic routes
  • Inaccuracies due to vessel transponder issues
  • Temporary unavailability due to service interruptions

Vessel positions shown in the App are approximate and should not be relied upon for time-critical decisions.
